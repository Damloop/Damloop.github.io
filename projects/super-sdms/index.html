Super SDMS – Comic Project for Hewlett‑Packard

Super SDMS is a promotional comic project developed for Hewlett‑Packard in December 2001, focused on cybersecurity and protection against computer viruses.

The comic was distributed together with promotional chocolates at two major events: the SIMO fair in Madrid and the Fira / Zoo Aquarium in Barcelona.

I was responsible for the drawing, aesthetics, visual narrative, and composition.
