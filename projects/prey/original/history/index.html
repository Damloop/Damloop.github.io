STORY & LORE

Humanity stands on the brink of extinction. The undead rise from their graves with insatiable hunger, and no one has survived… except you. As the Soldier Without a Name (SWN), you must carve your path through hordes of zombies, uncover ancient secrets, and face the horrors unleashed upon the Middle Earth. This is the legend of PREY — a world drowned in fog, cursed by the Ancients, and haunted by The Void.
