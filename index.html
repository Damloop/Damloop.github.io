Super SDMS – Comic Project for Hewlett‑Packard

Super SDMS is a promotional comic project developed for Hewlett‑Packard in December 2001, focused on cybersecurity and protection against computer viruses.

Creation of the comic character, visual design, and development of communication pieces and merchandising for events and the promotional campaign “Super SDMS,” a security service initiative.
